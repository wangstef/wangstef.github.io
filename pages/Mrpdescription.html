I am creating an interactive narrative clickthrough "game" comprised of modules geared towards parents of infants with Craniosynostosis. Craniosynostosis is a skull condition in which the skull shape becomes deformed due to early fusing of the skull sutures. This condition can only be treated with surgical intervention, specifically two different approaches. My resource lets parents navigate both of the two surgical options while learning about craniosynostosis.The resource will be an empathetic approach to educating and informing parents to aid their decisions regarding care for their infant. ...
