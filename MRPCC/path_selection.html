Before 4 months old

Endoscopic Strip Craniectomy

Surgery before 4 months old

This surgery is minimally invasive where 2 small cuts are made on the child's head. It requires fairly intensive after care (many in-person check ups and helmet care).

All ages

Cranial Vault Reshaping

Surgery typically before 12 months old

This is a more invasive surgery that remodels the skull. It requires less intensive aftercare and less in-person check ups.
